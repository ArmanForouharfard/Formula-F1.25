2025 SEASON PREVIEW

Technical Changes

The 2025 season introduces new aerodynamic regulations aimed at improving overtaking opportunities and reducing the "dirty air" effect that makes following other cars difficult.

  • Simplified front wing designs
  • Revised rear wing configurations
  • New sustainable fuel requirements

Calendar Highlights

The 2025 calendar features 24 races, including the return of the South African Grand Prix and a new street circuit in Seoul, South Korea.
